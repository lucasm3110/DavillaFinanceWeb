Real Estate Investments Bookkeeping

Streamlined accounting solutions for all your real estate financial needs.

At Davila Finance, we understand that successful real estate investment and development go hand-in-hand with meticulous financial management. That's why we offer specialized bookkeeping services tailored for the real estate sector. Our comprehensive services are designed to provide you with peace of mind, ensuring that all your financial records are accurate, up-to-date, and compliant.

    Comprehensive Bookkeeping Solutions
    Our real estate bookkeeping services include:

  • Bank Reconciliation: Regular reconciliation of your bank statements to ensure accurate financial records and timely identification of any discrepancies.
  • Daily Actualization of QuickBooks Software: Consistent updating of your QuickBooks accounts to reflect all transactions, keeping your financial data current and reliable.
  • Invoice Tracking: Rigorous following of invoices paid to builders, ensuring accurate expense recording and timely payments.
  • Construction Loans Draw Preparation: Compilation and submission of construction draw requests in alignment with project timelines, facilitating seamless financing throughout the construction phase.
  • Draw Scheduling: Regular monitoring and updating of draw schedules to optimize cash flow and meet project milestones.
  • Lien Waivers Compilation: Preparation and tracking of lien waivers, ensuring legal compliance and smooth transactions with builders and construction lenders.
  • Deed Records Management: Diligent tracking of deed records to confirm property ownership and other legal matters are in order.
  • Document Scanning and Storage: Secure digitization and storage of important financial documents, providing easy access and retrieval when needed.
  • County Permits Management: Tracking and following up on necessary county permits, ensuring compliance with local construction and zoning laws.
  • Communication with Construction Lenders: Effective liaison with construction lenders to ensure all financial requirements are met and maintained.
  • Tax Compilation to CPA and Auditors: Preparing and organizing all necessary financial data for tax compilation, making the year-end tax process more efficient and less stressful.

Streamlined Operations, Better Decision-Making Our team of experienced bookkeepers works seamlessly with your existing processes, leveraging state-of-the-art software and best practices. This not only simplifies your day-to-day operations but also provides you with the financial insights needed to make informed decisions.
