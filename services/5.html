Traditional and Non-Traditional Mortgage Loans

Offering a broad range of mortgage options to fit every need, from fixed-rate to adjustable, jumbo to VA/FHA/USDA loans.

In the ever-evolving landscape of real estate financing, Davila Finance offers an extensive range of mortgage loan options to suit various needs. Whether you're looking for a traditional mortgage to purchase your dream home or considering alternative lending options, we have you covered.

    Traditional Mortgage Loans For those seeking a straightforward, long-term financing solution, we offer a suite of traditional mortgage loans. These commonly include:

  • Fixed-Rate Mortgages: Lock in a constant interest rate for the life of the loan, offering predictability in your monthly payments.
  • Adjustable-Rate Mortgages (ARMs): Benefit from lower initial rates that may adjust over time based on market conditions.
  • FHA Loans: Ideal for first-time homebuyers, requiring lower down payments and accommodating less-than-perfect credit.
  • Conventional Loans: With stricter credit requirements but more flexible terms, these loans are often best suited for those with strong financial profiles.
  • Traditional mortgages are well-suited for individuals with stable income, good credit, and a willingness to commit to longer-term financial obligations.
    Non-Traditional Mortgage Loans For clients with unique financial circumstances or those looking for more flexible lending options, we offer non-traditional mortgage loans, such as:

  • Interest-Only Mortgages: Make only interest payments for a set period, allowing lower initial monthly payments.
  • Balloon Mortgages: Make smaller initial payments with a large lump sum due at the end of the loan term.
  • Stated Income Loans: Suitable for self-employed or freelance individuals, these loans are approved based on a declaration of income rather than traditional proof.
  • Hard Money Loans: These are asset-based loans that are ideal for short-term real estate investment opportunities.
  • Non-traditional loans are excellent solutions for those with variable incomes, investment-oriented financial goals, or unique property types.
    Mortgage Loans for International Customers and Non-Residents Understanding the unique financial needs of international customers and non-residents, Davila Finance has crafted mortgage loan products tailored just for you. These options make it simpler for non-residents and international investors to acquire real estate properties in the United States.
  • Foreign National Loans: Designed for non-U.S. citizens who are looking to invest in the American real estate market. Minimal documentation and flexible approval criteria set these loans apart.
  • Non-Resident Portfolio Loans: A bespoke financing solution that accommodates the unique income structures of non-residents, such as income from international sources.
  • Cross-Border Financing: Tailored for those who have financial profiles in multiple countries, these loans take into account international credit reports and assets.
  • Currency Choice Mortgages: Choose the currency in which you'd like to make your mortgage payments, providing a hedge against currency fluctuations.
